What's a bill of sale with a promissory note for an automobile?

Congratulations, you've found a new owner for your ride. Now, let's finalize the salea bill of sale transfers ownership from the seller to the buyer. A promissory note is a promise to pay. So, a bill of sale for an automobile with a promissory note is what you might expect from the (very long) name: A certification someone has bought and promises to pay for your car. In this case, it is likely in monthly installments.

Here's the information you'll need to have handy to complete your bill of sale with a promissory note for an automobile:

  • Who the buyer is: Have the name and contact information ready
  • Who the seller is: Have the name and contact information ready
  • What kind of car is it: Know the make, model, body type, year, and VIN of the car
  • How much the monthly payments will be: Know how much you want them to pay monthly and on what date you want to be paid
